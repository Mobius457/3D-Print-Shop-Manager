Welcome to PrintShopManager!
============================
This tool helps you track your 3D printing inventory and calculate costs.

GETTING STARTED:
1. Open the "Inventory" tab.
2. Click "Add New Spool" to log your first filament.
3. Go to the "Calculator" to price out a job.

OPTIONAL AI FEATURES:
This app has "Smart" features powered by Google Gemini (AI).
These are OFF by default. To enable them:
1. Get a FREE API Key from: https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey
2. In the app, click "Settings" -> Paste your key.
   (OR just click any AI button and it will ask you for it).

TROUBLESHOOTING:
- If the app doesn't open, ensure you unzipped the entire folder.
- Data is saved automatically to 'filament_inventory.json' in this folder.
